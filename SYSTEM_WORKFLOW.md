# 🤖 Recruit AI Agent: Simple Guide for Recruiters

Welcome to your AI-powered recruitment assistant! This document explains how the system works, what it does for you, and the "magic" happening behind the scenes—all in plain English.

---

## 🌟 What is this system?
Think of this as a **super-intelligent assistant** that never gets tired. It can read hundreds of resumes in seconds, understand which candidates are truly qualified, and help you focus your energy only on the "Top Stars."

---

## 🗺️ The Full Workflow (Step-by-Step)

### 1. Setting the Stage (Input)
*   **Job Description (JD):** You tell the AI what you're looking for. You can either upload a JD file or let the AI **generate one for you** based on a few keywords.
*   **The Candidates:** You can upload resume PDFs directly, or better yet, **Sync with Gmail**. The system will scan your inbox for applications automatically!

### 2. The Great Filter (Automated Screening)
Before you even see a candidate, the AI applies two strict rules to save your time:
*   **The "Length" Rule:** If a junior candidate (<3 years experience) has a 5-page resume, it’s rejected immediately. We keep it professional and concise!
*   **The "Role Match" Rule:** The AI does a quick check. If someone applied for a "Chef" role with a "Software Engineer" resume, they are skipped instantly.

### 3. Ranking the Best (Semantic Matching)
The system looks at all the valid candidates and ranks them from #1 to #100. It doesn't just look for keywords; it understands **meaning**. If you need a "Python Expert," it knows that someone with "Django experience" is a great match even if the word "Expert" isn't there.

### 4. The "Deep Dive" (AI Analysis)
Once the list is ranked, you decide how many "Top Candidates" you want to see (e.g., the Top 5). 
*   The AI then performs a **Deep Brain Scan** on these top profiles.
*   It identifies specific **Strengths**, **Weaknesses**, and even **Hidden Achievements**.
*   It gives every top candidate a final "Score" out of 100%.

### 5. Final Verdict (Next Steps)
*   **Selected:** The true winners stay at the top. You can send them an **Assessment Link** with one click.
*   **Not Selected:** For those who were good but didn't make the final cut, the system can send **Automated, Polite Rejection Emails** so no candidate is left wondering.

## 🧬 The Backend Deep Dive: How Shortlisting Works

Ever wondered exactly how the AI decides who makes the cut? The system uses a **4-layer filter** to ensure zero mistakes.

### **Layer 1: The "Clean Resume" Filter**
Before the AI reads anything deeply, it checks for professionalism:
*   **Junior Talent (< 3 Years Experience):** Must keep it to **1 page**. Anything longer is rejected for lack of focus.
*   **Senior Professionals (>= 3 Years Experience):** Can have **up to 2 pages**. Beyond that, the system skips it.

### **Layer 2: The Role-Scan (High-Speed Check)**
The backend runs a "Rapid Scan" on every resume to ensure the role matches. 
*   If you're hiring a **Marketing Manager** and someone applies with a **Gym Instructor** resume, the AI sees a low match score (less than 60%) and skips it immediately. This happens in milliseconds!

### **Layer 3: The Ranking Game (Semantic Sorting)**
The system ranks the remaining resumes from #1 to #100. It doesn't just look for words like "Python"; it understands that someone who builds "intelligent bots" is likely a "Python Developer." 
*   **The Buffer Logic:** If you tell the system you want the **Top 5** candidates, it doesn't just look at the first 5. It takes the **Top 10** to perform a "Deep Brain Scan" to find the absolute best among them.

### **Layer 4: The AI "Brain Scan" (Final Scoring)**
This is where the most advanced AI (GPT-4o) reads the top group line-by-line:
1.  **Strengths & Weaknesses:** It identifies precisely why someone is a good or bad fit.
2.  **Evidence-Based Scoring:** It gives a final score (0-100%) based on real experience, not just buzzwords.
3.  **The Victory Lap:** The very best candidates from this group are marked as **Selected** and get your assessment link!

### **Layer 5: The Automatic Closure**
Once the AI is done, the backend finishes the job:
*   **Selected Candidates:** Receive a tailored "Next Steps" email with an Assessment link.
*   **Close-Matches:** Those who were analyzed but didn't quite make the top cut receive a **Polite Rejection** email automatically.
*   **Irrelevant Resumes:** Those rejected at the very beginning (by the "Hard Rules" or "Role Scan") are simply ignored—no emails are sent, keeping your brand communication clean.
